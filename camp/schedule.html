The Schedule

A quick note on travel arrangements

When booking your travel, please be careful to allow sufficient driving time to our remote destination. You should plan to arrive between 4:00 - 6:30pm on Friday and depart around 11:00am on Monday. (Official discussions end by 7:00pm on Sunday if you must leave us early, but we really hope you'll stick around through Monday breakfast.)

The airports nearest to the ranch are:
· San Luis Obispo (SBP) - Approximately 1 hour
· Los Angeles (LAX) - Approximately 3 - 4.5 hours depending on traffic
· San Francisco (SFO) - Approximately 4.5 hours, but an easier drive than from LA
